About Circular Shift

Cradle to Cradle Products Innovation Institute is organising Circular Shift, an annual summit held in Amsterdam and New York. The event explores best practices and new pathways for powering innovation for a safe and circular future.

Read more about our organization

10 Speakers

We have gathered 10 prominent speakers from all across the world in the field of circularity, sustainability and modularity. We will engage with them to pick their brains about trends and technologies that will shape our near future.

250+ Attendees

Join top brands, designers, manufacturers, and leaders from the sustainability, policy and finance sectors for keynotes, conversations and practical discussions focused on the roadmap to a circular economy.
